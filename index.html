Welcome to Nvidia

The company was founded in 1993 by three American computer scientists, Jen-Hsun Huang, Curtis Priem, and Christopher Malachowsky. NVIDIA is known for developing integrated circuits, which are used in everything from electronic game consoles to personal computers (PCs).
